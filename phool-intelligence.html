    Strategic Insights

    Key findings from the data

    Actionable pricing intelligence drawn from the dataset — designed to inform product and pricing decisions for a new ethnic collection launch.

    👗
    Anarkali commands a 30% price premium

    Anarkali silhouettes average ₹881 vs ₹679 for A-Line. Straight shape sits at a mid-range ₹774. Silhouette choice is one of the strongest value signals in the category.

    ✂️
    Hemline is the single strongest price driver

    At 32% feature importance, hemline finishing has the greatest influence on price. Curved and Flared hemlines average ₹900+ compared to ₹738 for Straight — a design investment worth the tailoring cost.

    🧵
    Long sleeves justify higher margins

    Long-sleeved dresses average ₹833 vs ₹588 for Sleeveless — a ₹245 premium attributable to fabric quantity and construction complexity. Cotton and Georgette currently outperform Silk in this dataset.

    💰
    Price 15–25% above benchmark for boutique positioning

    Boutique advantages — personalisation, custom sizing, and artisanal quality — justify pricing at benchmark + 15% as a standard rate and + 30% for bespoke or custom-fit orders.

    📐
    Wider size ranges support premium positioning

    Offering a broader size range signals inclusivity and quality focus. Each additional size tier adds a measurable, if modest, price uplift in the trained model.

    🌸
    Floral is the category norm — differentiate by cut

    94% of Myntra ethnic kurtas feature floral print. Pattern alone does not command a premium. Distinctive silhouette — Anarkali with a curved hem and long sleeves — is what drives pricing separation.
